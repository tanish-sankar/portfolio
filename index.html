About

Hey — I'm Tanish Thiyagasankar, a student with a passion for making things at the edge of art and technology. Whether I'm sketching concepts on paper or building functional web interfaces, I care deeply about both aesthetics and execution.

My work spans UI/UX design, front-end development, and digital art. I believe the best projects happen when visual thinking and technical skill work together — not in isolation.

This portfolio is a living document of my growth. Every project here represents a problem I found interesting, a skill I was developing, or an idea I needed to get out of my head and into the world.
